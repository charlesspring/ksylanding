About Us

We're a team of believers, believe that good things will happen.

Founded in 2014

Skyisland Inc. is situated in Handan, Hebei, China—a small town that has retained its name for over three thousand years. This charming location seamlessly blends ancient and modern cultures, creating a unique tapestry of tradition and innovation. Notably, it is also the birthplace of the renowned science fiction novel "The Three-Body Problem" which draws inspiration from the author's encounter with a rare phenomenon known as ball lightning.
